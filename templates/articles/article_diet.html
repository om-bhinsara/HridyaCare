Best Foods for Heart Health

What you eat has a direct impact on your blood pressure, cholesterol, and overall heart resilience. A heart-healthy diet is not about strict limitations, but about prioritizing nutrient-dense fuel.

Pro Tip: Aim to fill half your plate with vegetables and fruits at every meal.
