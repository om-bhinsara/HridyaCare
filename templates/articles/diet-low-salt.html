Low-Salt Diet

Sodium acts like a sponge that holds water in your body. This extra water puts pressure on your blood vessel walls, forcing your heart to work harder. Reducing salt is the single most effective dietary change for lowering blood pressure.

Hidden Sodium

The salt shaker isn't the main culprit. 70% of sodium comes from packaged foods like bread, pizza, cold cuts, and canned soups. Always check the label!
