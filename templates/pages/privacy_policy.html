Your health data is sensitive. We've designed HridyaCare to prioritize your privacy through immediate data disposal and transparent processing.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized wellness experience, we collect specific data points:

  • Basic account details (Name, email, age, and city)
  • Heart-rate readings (Numerical BPM values only)
  • Environmental context (Local Air Quality Index)
  • Anonymous performance telemetry
Privacy Shield: HridyaCare never stores camera images, video frames, facial data, or raw biometric signals. All processing happens locally or in volatile memory.

2. How Camera Data Is Handled

Camera input is processed temporarily and in real time to estimate physiological signals. Once heart-rate values are calculated, all visual data is immediately purged from the system memory.

The camera is only used during active measurement sessions and is never accessed in the background or without your explicit permission.

3. Purpose of Data Usage

  • Generating wellness insights and trend summaries
  • Personalizing health reports and recommendations
  • Improving algorithm accuracy and reliability
  • Maintaining secure platform infrastructure

4. Medical & Wellness Disclaimer

HridyaCare is a wellness-awareness platform and is not a medical device. Insights provided are not intended for clinical diagnosis or treatment.

5. Storage & Retention

Data is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 protocols and retained only as long as your account remains active. You maintain full ownership of your data and may request total deletion at any time.

Right to Delete: You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.
